South Indian Recipes

Soft Idli

Steamed rice cakes — light and healthy breakfast option.

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Urad dal
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt
  • Water
Preparation:

Soak, grind, and ferment batter overnight. Steam in idli molds for 10–15 mins.

Crispy Masala Dosa

Crispy fermented rice-lentil crepe filled with spiced potato.

Ingredients:
  • Dosa batter
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onion, Mustard, Curry leaves
  • Turmeric, Green chili
Preparation:

Spread batter on tawa, fill with potato masala, fold and serve hot.

Traditional Sambar

A tangy lentil-based vegetable stew with spices and tamarind.

Ingredients:
  • Toor dal
  • Tamarind
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Sambar powder
  • Mustard, curry leaves
Preparation:

Cook dal and veggies, add tamarind and spices, simmer, and temper with mustard.
